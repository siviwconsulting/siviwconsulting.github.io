Analytical Inverse Analysis for Multilayered Medium Terrain

Authors: Bolatbek Rysbaiuly, Artem Sinitsa, Antonio Capsoni

Abstract: We demonstrate a novel inverse theory approach for the analytical estimation of thermo-physical properties in multi-layered media. Using homogenized experimental measurements, we derived exact analytical expressions to determine eight key physical parameters simultaneously.

The methodology allows for the decomposition of time-domains into quasi-stationary sub-intervals, overcoming the need for complex adjoint conjugate gradients while maintaining high accuracy.

Exact Estimation of Elasticity Parameters in Thermoelastic Stress Models

Authors: Artem V. Sinitsa, Antonio Capsoni

Abstract: This paper presents a well-posed mathematical model depicting general interconnections between physically governed fields for thermally heated plates. We demonstrate the derivation of the conjugate problem and analytical expressions used for exact estimation of structural material properties.

The novelty lies in the derivation of exact estimators utilizing variational calculus and fractional order derivatives, eliminating the need for expensive empirical laboratory sampling.
