Exact Estimation of Elasticity Parameters in Thermoelastic Stress Models

Authors: Artem V. Sinitsa, Antonio Capsoni

This paper presents a well-posed mathematical model depicting general interconnections between physically governed fields for thermally heated plates. The novelty lies in the derivation of exact estimators utilizing variational calculus and fractional order derivatives.

doi:10.3390/sym14112248

Analytical Inverse Analysis for Multilayered Medium Terrain

Authors: Bolatbek Rysbaiuly, Artem Sinitsa, Antonio Capsoni

We demonstrate a novel inverse theory approach for the analytical estimation of thermo-physical properties in multi-layered media. Using homogenized experimental measurements, we derived exact analytical expressions to determine eight key physical parameters simultaneously.
